## Dropout
<u>“Feature co-adaptation”</u> means neurons learn to be useful only together in very specific combinations, instead of being individually robust.

During training, <u>"dropout"</u> randomly turns off (sets to zero) some neurons on each forward pass.
- On every batch, you train a slightly different sub-network.
- At test time, dropout is turned off; you use the full network, usually with weights/outputs scaled appropriately.
- <u>Dropout</u> is a form of regularization
